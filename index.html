2025 Competition Details

The 2025 AABC competition is schedule for Saturday the 1st of Novemeber 2025. Entrants placing, 1st, 2nd or 3rd in qualifying state competitions are eligible to enter, in the same category as their state placing.

Qualifying entrants can register their entries at https://enter.aabc.asn.au
